Mission Architecture

Define mission goals, orbit assumptions, payload needs, subsystem boundaries, and verification plans.

02

Onboard Computing and Experimentation

Explore embedded processing, fault tolerance, data handling, and compute choices for constrained space systems.

03

Model satellite communication paths, ground interfaces, link budgets, and protocol-level constraints.

04

Control Systems

Study attitude control, sensing, actuation, and autonomy needed for stable mission operation.

05

Power

Plan generation, storage, regulation, and subsystem power budgets for reliable satellite operation.
